Our Story

From Pizza to Full-Service Catering

318 Food Co. grew from a love of serving great food into a broader catering company built to handle office lunches, parties, schools, churches, weddings and community events.

Our goal is simple: provide food people are excited to eat, show up prepared and make the entire process easy from the first conversation through the final setup.
