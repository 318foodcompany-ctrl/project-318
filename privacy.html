Privacy Policy

Effective July 18, 2026 · Consent version 2026-07-18

318 Food Co. uses information you provide to respond to catering inquiries, prepare quotes, manage bookings, issue invoices, receive payments, and provide customer service.

Information you provide

When you request a quote or contact us, we may collect your name, contact details, event information, guest count, menu preferences, service needs, and other information you choose to provide.

Website attribution data

Our website may store anonymous first-party identifiers and limited campaign information such as referral source, landing page, UTM campaign fields, and advertising click identifiers. This information helps us understand which marketing source led to a catering inquiry. It is not used by itself to identify you.

Optional analytics and advertising

Analytics and advertising technologies are disabled by default. They may be activated only after you grant the applicable permission through our privacy choices banner. You may accept, reject, change, or withdraw optional consent at any time by selecting “Privacy settings” in the website footer.

Consent records

Your choices are stored in your browser with a consent version, timestamp, and expiration date. We may ask you to choose again when the policy changes or the saved preference expires.

How information is shared

We may use service providers that support website hosting, customer management, invoicing, payment processing, analytics, advertising, email, and other business operations. We do not sell catering inquiry information.

Retention and security

We retain information for as long as reasonably needed to provide services, maintain business and tax records, resolve disputes, and protect the business and our customers. We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards, but no online system can guarantee absolute security.

Your choices

  • Use the footer privacy settings control to change optional analytics or advertising consent.
  • Contact us to ask about, correct, or request deletion of personal information, subject to legal and business record requirements.
  • Adjust browser settings to remove locally stored website data.

Contact us

Questions about this policy may be sent to 318FoodCompany@gmail.com or by calling (318) 572-0137.
